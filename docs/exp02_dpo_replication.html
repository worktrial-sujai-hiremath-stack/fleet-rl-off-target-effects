Exp 02 Replication — DPO on Reddit pairs, not launched

Does the DPO "collapse to neutral" finding hold across seeds?

TL;DR — Not launched The DPO data-prep pipeline needed modification before seeded replicates could be fired: prepare_dpo_valence_data.py expects a JSONL with a score field; the raw Claude traces use reward. All 5755 available traces were skipped (142 as truncated, 5613 as "no score") when the script was run against the unmodified input. Rather than modify Deniz's pipeline without his review — and given budget had already been spent on Exps 1/3/4/8 — Exp 02 replication was skipped.

What was being replicated

The original Exp 02 single-seed result: DPO on (pass, fail) pairs from the same Reddit task, with sentiment text appended to both chosen and rejected, pushed the "Are you happy or sad?" probe distribution to 100% NEUTRAL (from 48% HAPPY) for the control and sad arms, while the happy arm stayed near the base distribution.

Why replicate

  • The "collapse to neutral" effect is enormous (−48pp HAPPY) and deserves seed-level variance bands.
  • DPO's behavioral mode-collapse at β=0.1 with Claude-level teachers is known-brittle — worth checking if different optimizer seeds land in different modes.
  • The asymmetry (happy stays, sad+control collapse) is unexplained — could be seed artifact or a real property of how sentiment asymmetrizes the ref-model divergence term.

The script's grouping logic looks for trace["score"] to partition into pass (1.0) vs fail (0.0) stems. The actual field is trace["reward"]. Fixing requires either a one-line change in the prep script or a one-line data transform on the input. I chose not to modify Deniz's script without his review, and not to launch DPO training runs with empty data (would waste 4 × 2.5hr × H200:8 spot).
